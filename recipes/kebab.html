Kebab

This kebab sandwich is my version of Turkish kebabs, which I made 'off the cuff' after searching unsuccessfully for a recipe online.
The original sandwiches are made from meat pressed into a loaf and grilled on a rotating spit.
The meat is shaved off as it gets crispy and put into a pita sandwich.
