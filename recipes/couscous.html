Couscous

This recipe is made with mutton and chicken, but you can easily change the meats for lamb and/or merguez.
make this often and my family loves it!

  • 3 carrots, sliced lengthwise and cut into chunks
  • 2 zucchini, sliced lengthwise and cut into chunks

    Steps

  1. Step 1

    Heat 3 tablespoons olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Season mutton chops and chicken drumsticks with salt and pepper;
    cook in batches with onions in the hot oil until browned, about 2 minutes per side. Transfer to a large plate.

  2. Step 2

    Scrape the bottom of the pot with a wooden spoon to release browned bits. Return mutton chops and chicken to the pot. Pour in enough water to cover;
    add turmeric, cumin, and coriander. Cover and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium; simmer for 20 minutes.
